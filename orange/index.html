Orange's'


2021


Video

A short stop-motion animated film for the charity brand Buy42.
The author was moved by the story of the disabled clerk in the Buy42 charity store to make this video. The video tells the story of a flawed orange who accidentally exposed his flaws in a cautious life, was squeezed out, sad and lonely, and finally accepted by Buy42 and lived a happy life. Convey the inclusive brand concept.
